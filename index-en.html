Structured Foundational Document

Technical consolidation of the assessment performed, in written document, with identification of risks, sensitive points, and prioritized next steps.

Institutional Traceability and Registration

Documentary structuring focused on traceability, historical organization, and possibility of intellectual registration of the project, methodology, or legal structure.

Registration as a layer of protection and institutional memory.

Investment Perspective Assessment

Evaluation of legal and decision-making structure according to criteria usually observed by funds, institutional investors, and due diligence processes.
